Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how reloo (the "Application") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Application. The Application is provided by Francisco Quezada (the "Service Provider").

Summary: We collect only the information needed to operate the Application, including account sign-in identifiers (Apple/Google), optional profile information, event content you submit (including photos), approximate or precise location (if you grant permission), and purchase status for in-app purchases. We use third-party services such as Supabase (backend/storage), Mapbox (maps), Apple/Google (sign-in), and Apple App Store / Google Play (payments).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account and profile information: When you sign in, we may receive an identifier from Apple or Google. You may also provide optional profile details (e.g., display name).
  • User-generated content: When you create events, you may submit text, dates/times, locations, links, and photos/images. This content is stored so it can be displayed in the Application.
  • Support communications: If you contact us, we may collect the information you include in your message.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Location information (with permission): If you grant location permission, we may collect approximate or precise location to show nearby events and improve map-based features. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • Device and usage information: We may collect basic device information (e.g., device model, OS version) and usage data necessary for performance and troubleshooting.
  • Push notification token (optional): If you enable notifications, we may collect a device push token to send notifications. You can disable notifications in your device settings.
  • Purchase status: If you make an in-app purchase, we may store purchase/subscription status (e.g., active/inactive) to enable paid features. We do not collect or store full payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the Application (sign-in, event discovery, event creation, and display of content).
  • Show nearby events and location-based features (when location permission is granted).
  • Store and display user-generated event content, including photos.
  • Enable and manage paid features by verifying purchase status (in-app purchases).
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and maintain security.
  • Communicate with you when you contact support.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to provide the Application, comply with law, or protect rights and safety.

3.1 Service providers and platforms

  • Supabase: We use Supabase as our backend/database and file storage to store event data, user records, and uploaded photos.
  • Mapbox: We use Mapbox to display maps and map-related content. Map requests may include approximate location information or map coordinates necessary to render the map and show nearby events.
  • Apple and Google (Sign-In): We use Apple/Google authentication to allow you to sign in. These providers process authentication according to their own policies.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (Payments): In-app purchases are processed by the platform stores. We receive purchase status information to unlock features, but we do not receive your full payment details.

3.2 Legal and safety

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the Service Provider, or the public.

4. Your Choices

  • Location: You can grant or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings.
  • Notifications: You can enable/disable notifications through your device settings.
  • Account information: You may update certain profile details in the Application (if available).

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Application and for legitimate business purposes (such as maintaining records, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements), unless a longer retention period is required by law. User-generated content (including photos) may remain available until you delete it (where available) or request removal.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. International Users

If you use the Application from outside the country where we operate, your information may be processed and stored in other countries where our service providers (such as Supabase) operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards where required.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the Application after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
